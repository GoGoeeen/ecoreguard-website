Frequently asked questions

What exactly is the EmpCo Directive? expand_more
The EmpCo Directive (EU 2024/825) - fully titled "Empowering Consumers for the Green Transition" - extends existing unfair commercial practices rules with clear constraints for sustainability claims. Member states had to transpose it by March 27, 2026, and the new rules apply from September 27, 2026.
Does EmpCo also affect B2B companies? expand_more
While primarily aimed at B2C, many member states apply it to B2B. Germany and Italy already treat B2B sustainability claims with similar rigor. Suppliers to B2C brands must also prepare to provide verifiable proof to their clients.
What happens if my company fails to meet requirements? expand_more
Sanctions include fines up to 4% of annual turnover, injunctions from competitors/NGOs, and mandatory withdrawal of products from the EU market, alongside severe reputational damage.
How does flustix certification help? expand_more
flustix provides independent, third-party verification that your sustainability claims are based on recognized systems. This directly fulfills EmpCo's requirement for verified sustainability labels.
